SAPFLUXNET: A global database of sap flow measurements

SAPFLUXNET Data Upload Form

  • SAPFLUXNET is an initiative aiming at compiling a global database of sap flow measurements in plants led by CREAF and an international team of researchers. The main goal of SAPFLUXNET is to advance in the scientific understanding of the ecological factors driving plant transpiration across the globe. Please see here for more information about the project.
  • We invite the sap flow community worldwide to contribute with datasets to SAPFLUXNET. The basic requirements for data contribution are:
    • Whole-plant sap flow time series must have been measured during at least one year/growing season, ideally at sub-daily resolution.
    • The experimental site must be a field setting (including orchards, but excluding studies in greenhouses and in potted plants).
    • Site- and tree-level metadata must be provided, meeting the minimum requirements specified in the metadata spreadsheet.
    • Ancillary time series data must include basic meteorological monitoring spanning the entire duration of the sap flow measuring period.
